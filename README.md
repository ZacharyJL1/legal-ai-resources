# legal-ai-resources
Curated resources, workflows, and contributions for AI adoption in the legal community.

## Overview

This repository is maintained by an attorney and active contributor in the r/legaltech community. It aims to help legal professionals adopt AI tools responsibly and effectively.

## Contents

- **Guides and workflows:** Step-by-step instructions on integrating AI assistants (like Claude, GPT-4, etc.) into legal tasks such as research, drafting, and summarization.
- **Resource list:** Links to articles, tools, and discussions that help lawyers understand AI adoption.
- **Community contributions:** Summaries of discussions and common questions from the r/legaltech community to guide developers on what features lawyers need.
- **Feedback for developers:** Insights from practicing lawyers on how AI tools could improve legal workflows.

## Contributing

Contributions are welcome! If you have experience with AI in legal practice or want to suggest resources, feel free to open a pull request or start a discussion.
